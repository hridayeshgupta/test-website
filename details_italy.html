Italy Tour Details

Italy Holiday Packages

The country of Italy epitomises the holiday destination of dreams. It is a nation that is defined by magnificent cities, ancient sights, cultural treasures, and beautiful scenery. There are so many choices for travellers; it's simply hard to know where to begin. To explore the most of what Italy has on offer, getting hands on an Italy tour package can be extremely beneficial. Quite evidently, Italy has the largest number of UNESCO World Heritage sites in the planet, and has often been named as one big living museum. It’s also one of the world’s most frequented tourist destinations for its northern mountains, the Eternal City of Rome, Venice- the City of Romance, and of course, its breathtaking coastlines. Whatever makes a perfect vacation, Italy has it, with amazing food and wine, warm and sunny summers, colourful festivals and scintillating nightlife as a bonus. Its premier attractions are world famous for their association with the Roman Era and the iconic Renaissance period. Exploring Coliseum in Rome, Grand Canal in Rialtine Islands, and the treasures of Florence and Naples are all-time favourites with visitors. The country is not just about ancient ruins, but stunning beaches, exciting water sports, wildlife sanctuaries, outdoor activities and waterside taverns.

Sailing on Italy’s expansive coastline and water sports of all kinds are popular outdoor activities to indulge in when on an Italy tour. Wherever one puts up in Italy, there’ll be fascinating places to see and things to do. In the north, there are alpine lakes, snow-swathed mountains, age-old towns and the glories of Venice. Rome, Florence, Sicily, Vesuvius, Naples, beaches and breathtaking countryside, among many other destinations command attention of tourists. If the picturesque island of Sicily is part of one's Italy holiday package, he/she must not miss to head to regional park, Etna. The park is endowed with beautiful walking paths in addition to winter ski trails. Its fertile volcanic soil creates an amazing landscape that is rich in foliage, wildlife and bird life. Whether one is staying in a five-star resort or a locally-owned riverside hotel with a few rooms to rent, he/she is sure to get immersed in the Italian community in no time. Eating out and accommodation are affordable in the country. Concisely, there’s an endless choice of destinations fringed across the mainland and the islands of Italy, catering for every style of vacation, whether for beach-hopping, family holidaying or a romantic getaway.
